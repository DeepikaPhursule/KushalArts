Project Description

'The Leela Palace' is a 5 Star Hotel at New Delhi & Chennai. Wordone from 2009 to 2012.

Project Details

  • Work completed as per the time estimate.
  • Worked with artist working with fibre and clay.
  • Work process - Clay modelling, fibre work, fibre casting, fibre production and painting.
